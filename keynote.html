Honored Keynote Speakers

John McAfee

John McAfee was born September 18, 1945 and is the founder of McAfee anti-virus protections systems. He has spent most of his life being a computer programmer as well as a general business man. Many of his programs are still being used not only within the continental United States, but also internationally. He has become an ideal for many people seeking cyber security career fields. He will also be teaching a few workshops this year.

Leonard Bosack

Leonard Bosack was born in 1952 and was the original cofounder of Cisco Systems along with his wife Sandy Lerner. Cisco is one of the main networking components of the networking world that we live in today. Cisco started inside of a garage when it first began but was able to land very large contracts with the government shortly after. Mr. Bosack will also be seen teaching courses around Networks and Network Security.
